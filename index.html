About Our Product

EcoPins are reusable velcro pins that support environmental sustainability. Our mission is to create eco-friendly accessories while raising awareness about endangered species in Nova Scotia.

Nova Scotia Wildlife Collection

Support local endangered species through our special designs. Each pin represents an animal that needs our protection.

North Atlantic Right Whale
Whale Design Image
One of the world's most endangered whale species, with only about 350 remaining.
Population: Only about 350 remaining
Habitat: Nova Scotia waters
Status: Critically Endangered
Support marine conservation, reduce plastic use, choose sustainable seafood, report whale sightings, AND BUY A WHALE PIN FROM US!
Canada Lynx
Lynx Design Image
Threatened species in Nova Scotia forests, particularly in Cape Breton Highlands.
Habitat: Cape Breton Highlands
Diet: Snowshoe hares
Status: Threatened
Support forest conservation, respect wildlife habitats, donate to protection programs, learn about coexistence, AND BUY A LYNX PIN FROM US!
Leatherback Sea Turtle
Turtle Design Image
Endangered ocean travelers that migrate thousands of miles through our waters.
Size: Up to 2,000 pounds
Migration: Thousands of miles
Status: Endangered
Reduce plastic pollution, support turtle rescue centers, participate in beach cleanups, choose reef-safe sunscreen, AND BUY A TURTLE PIN FROM US!
