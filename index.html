About EcoPins

EcoPins are reusable velcro pins designed to support environmental sustainability and conservation efforts. Each pin represents an endangered species from Nova Scotia, with proceeds going to wildlife protection programs.

Nova Scotia Wildlife Collection

Support local endangered species through our special animal designs

North Atlantic Right Whale
One of the world's most endangered whale species, found in Nova Scotia waters.
Population: Only about 350 remaining
Habitat: Nova Scotia waters
Status: Critically Endangered
Threats: Ship strikes, fishing gear entanglement
Buy a whale pin to support marine conservation efforts!
Canada Lynx
Threatened species in Nova Scotia forests, particularly in Cape Breton Highlands.
Habitat: Cape Breton Highlands
Diet: Snowshoe hares
Status: Threatened
Population: Fewer than 50 in Nova Scotia
Purchase a lynx pin to help protect forest habitats!
Leatherback Sea Turtle
Endangered ocean travelers that visit Nova Scotia waters during migration.
Size: Up to 2,000 pounds
Migration: Thousands of miles
Status: Endangered
Lifespan: Up to 50 years
Get a turtle pin to support ocean conservation!
